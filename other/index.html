Other Projects

TATTOO DESIGN

A friend of mine asked me to design his first tattoo for him. The text in the tattoo is written in Gujarati, “Mara naam ne yogya chhu” meaning “I am worthy of my name”. My friend is named after the Hindu god Arjuna, a brave and unwavering archer, who inspired the arrow motif. My friend often will look to his namesake in times of struggle for motivation to persevere. The circular symbol in the center is the Ashoka chakra, which has 24 spokes symbolizing the hours of the day and the movement of time. It also symbolizes the importance and growth that comes from change, encouraging us not to stagnate.

BATTERY LINE REDESIGN

I was given the task of redesigning a battery line during a co-op at Tesla Toronto Automation (formerly Hibar Systems Ltd). The battery line blocked the corridor, limiting the use of forklifts in the factory. To resolve this issue, new rails were added in at an angle to correct the position of the equipment. I designed an assembly of over 75 parts, made new drawings and a BOM. I oversaw the manufacturing and project management to ensure all parts were made, labelled, and shipped to the factory in China.
